# genhaz: General Hazard Models for Survival Analysis

Fits penalized cubic spline generalized hazards (GH) models for survival
data, encompassing proportional hazards (PH), accelerated failure time
(AFT), additive hazards (AH), and the full GH specification. Smoothing
parameter selection via a modified leave-one-out cross-validation (LCV)
criterion. Post-estimation inference includes pointwise delta-method
confidence intervals for the hazard, survival, cumulative hazard,
restricted mean survival time (RMST), and their between-group
differences. Also provides mixture Weibull utilities for data generation
and simulation studies.
